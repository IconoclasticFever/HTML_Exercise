Anton Byers

Anton was born a small black girl in Alabama in 1930. From an early age Anton preferred the company of chickens to humans and this would foretell his future calling as a pioneer rock and roll songwriter, writing amongst many other hits, the classic, "Chicken Scratch Fever".
